## 2. The Science: How NAC Works in Your Muscles
- Glutathione depletion during intense exercise
- NAC increases muscle cysteine availability
- Supports maintenance of glutathione levels during training

## 3. Performance Benefits: What the Research Shows
- Improved time to fatigue in endurance activities
- Enhanced recovery between high-intensity efforts
- Reduced exercise-induced muscle soreness
- Benefits primarily in endurance-trained individuals
- Effects on ionic shifts during intense exercise

## 4. Who Benefits Most from NAC Supplementation
- Key finding: Works best in individuals with low baseline glutathione
- May help older lifters with age-related glutathione decline
- Endurance athletes vs. strength athletes - different effects
- The "individual response" factor in antioxidant supplementation
